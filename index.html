Cloud PC Onboarding

Welcome to Your Yazaki Cloud PC

This guide will walk you step‑by‑step through setting up and using your Yazaki Cloud PC on a Chromebook. It is intended for new employees joining Yazaki, and existing employees transitioning from a traditional laptop or desktop to the Cloud PC environment.

The Cloud PC allows you to securely access your work environment from anywhere while keeping Yazaki data protected.

Table of Contents

What is Cloud PC?

Cloud PC is a cloud‑based virtual desktop, giving secure access to your work environment from anywhere.

Think of Cloud PC like a streaming service (such as Netflix or Disney+), but for your work computer.

Instead of your work desktop—with all its files, applications, and software—being stored directly on your laptop or Chromebook, it “lives” on our company’s powerful and secure servers. When you log in, we “stream” that desktop directly to your screen.

Your Chrome OS / Chromebook acts as a secure window to your full‑featured work computer.

And Why Is It Great for You?

🖥️

Familiar desktop experience

It looks and feels just like your regular desktop at work.

🌍

Work From Anywhere

As long as you have your device and an internet connection, you can access your exact same work desktop, whether you are at the office, at home, or traveling.

🔒

Enhanced Security

Your work data is not stored on the device itself. This means if your device is ever lost, stolen, or fails, your sensitive information remains completely safe and secure on our servers.

⚡

Always Fast & Up‑to‑Date

You get the performance of a high‑end computer because all the heavy lifting is done by our powerful servers. Your applications will always be updated and ready to go.

✨

Simplicity

Your device is simple, secure, and starts up in seconds. All the complexity of a traditional computer is managed for you behind the scenes.

Before You Begin

Please make sure you have the following ready:

  • Your Yazaki email address and password
  • A stable internet connection (office or home Wi‑Fi)
  • A smartphone (required for Microsoft Authenticator setup for new users)
  • Any peripherals you plan to use (monitor, mouse, keyboard, headset, webcam)

Important: Your first login may take longer than usual while your Cloud PC finishes provisioning.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps in order to get your Yazaki Cloud PC fully set up.

1

Power On and Sign Into the Chromebook

  1. Turn on your Chromebook.
  2. At the login screen, select Yazaki.
2

Connect to Wi‑Fi

  1. Click the Wi‑Fi icon in the bottom‑right corner of the screen.
  2. Confirm you are connected to one of the following:
    • YZK‑Office (when onsite)
    • Your home Wi‑Fi network (when remote)

A strong and stable Wi‑Fi connection is critical for good Cloud PC performance.

3

Configure Monitors and Peripherals

  1. Open the Chromebook Settings menu.
  2. Navigate to Device → Displays to configure:
    • External monitors
    • Screen resolution and layout
  3. Navigate to Bluetooth / USB / Audio to configure:
    • Mouse
    • Keyboard
    • Headset
    • Webcam

Important Note: All physical devices (mouse, keyboard, headset, webcam, etc.) must be configured in the Chromebook settings. The Cloud PC will mirror the Chromebook’s internal setup.

4

Log Into Citrix

  1. Open the Citrix application or Citrix browser page.
  2. Sign in using your Yazaki email address and password.

Microsoft Authenticator (New Users Only)

If prompted:

  1. Install the Microsoft Authenticator app on your smartphone.
  2. Follow the on‑screen instructions to complete multi‑factor authentication (MFA).
5

Select the Correct Cloud PC Resource

If multiple Citrix resources are available:

  • Select DaaS YNCA Task User – USCA
This is the default and correct Cloud PC environment.
6

First‑Time Cloud PC Setup

Once your Cloud PC loads:

Verify OneDrive

OneDrive should automatically log into your Yazaki account and begin backing up or transferring your data.

Check for the blue cloud icon in the bottom right corner to see if you are logged in correctly. If the cloud is grey you may need to log in.

Store files in OneDrive folders (Desktop, Documents, Pictures) to ensure backup, recovery, and cross‑platform protection.

Open Outlook and Teams

  • Open Outlook and confirm your mailbox loads.
  • Open Microsoft Teams:
    • Verify audio, microphone, and camera settings
    • Perform a test call if needed

Pin Frequently Used Apps

  1. Open the applications you use regularly.
  2. Right‑click each app icon on the taskbar.
  3. Select Pin to taskbar.
7

Set Up Microsoft Edge

  1. Open Microsoft Edge.
  2. Confirm you are signed in with your Yazaki account.
  3. Verify bookmarks and favorites transferred correctly.
  4. Navigate to SuccessFactors and confirm access.

Microsoft Edge is the recommended and supported browser in the Cloud PC.

Daily Use Tips

  • Save all files to OneDrive
  • Lock or sign out when stepping away
  • Close Citrix properly at the end of the day

Logging Out vs Disconnecting

Disconnecting

Keeps your session running in the background.

Logging Out

Fully ends your session (recommended at end of day).

Basic Troubleshooting

Cloud PC feels slow

  • Check Wi‑Fi connection
  • Disconnect and reconnect Citrix

No sound / mic not working

  • Verify Chromebook audio device settings
  • Re‑select devices in Teams

Mouse / keyboard not working

  • Reconnect device in Chromebook settings

Authentication issues

  • Ensure Microsoft Authenticator notifications are enabled
